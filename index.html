About JOGL

Vision

Wishing to make research accessible to all who want to contribute, beyond the elitist academic and private laboratories, JOGL thinks a post-academic system facilitating responsible and inclusive innovation at all levels. This approach is intended for laboratories, NGOs, patient associations, social entrepreneurs and local authorities.

Concretely, JOGL gives the opportunity to all those who wish, to contribute or even to initiate scientific projects, alongside academic researchers. To achieve this goal, JOGL is the first fully open and distributed research institute with no physical space. This institute combines two aspects: a platform on which everyone can create a profile and a project, as well as participatory research programs around various issues.

Objectives

One critical objective of JOGL is to synchronize humanity to achieve long term goals.

Focus must be made on every tools, incentives and features which help collaboration support.

Main features

  1. Basic platform architecture

    A platform to manage the following objects:
    • Challenges
    • Projects
    • Profiles with skills
    • Organizations
    • Milestones (tasks categories?)
    • Tasks / Opportunities
    • Results
    • Events

  2. Interactive data visualization - contextualization in a big vast world

    Quick organizational mapping, to visualize:
    • Communities
    • people behind projects and challenges, how they relate to each other, and with other communities,
    • easily see the network of people,
    • Interactions and links between users
    • to see how am I related to the people and skills I need for my project,
    • or to meet the people I need to learn what I want to learn,
    • Support collaboration between users
    • with incentives to create and bind the adequate links with other people,
    • Skills within a community
    • to be able to quickly say which skill is missing,
    • to easily manage the group and allocate tasks,
    • Activity of users, projects, challenges and organizations
    • quickly see what happened relatively to those objects

  3. Services and data aggregator - JOGL as a platform of platforms

    External services to manage the projects and challenges, with two objectives:
    • reference all tools for a project (simple links to the tools)
    • automatically get / push data, for example:
    • auto-complete user profiles from LinkedIn profiles
    • auto-synchronize JOGL milestones and tasks from / to a project management platform like Jira, Taiga, Gitlab, Github, etc.

  4. Challenge object - syncing humanity for achieving long term impact

    In addition to the technological tools that JOGL will provide, there will be support to local communities, and strong incentives for collaboration to achieve big challenges. The goal is to gather communities and focus the best human resources to solve critical research goals.

  5. Smart compiler - The Brain

    Recommending content and actions for continuous learning and impact at individual or group scale. The objective is to help people and community to efficiently find what they need to achieve their research goals, provide all possible incentives to support projects and challenges.

    The recommendation systems will advise various objects:
    • Projects
    • Resources / Data
    • Content
    • Tasks / Opportunities

Contributing

Beta Committee Objectives

The Beta committee has two majors goals:
  1. JOGL as a project of JOGL: develop a common reflexion about interesting features for the next versions,
  2. Build a solid foundation to support the project: work on recruiting projects and partners.

Working as one

We are doing something that is wildly audacious, something that has not been done before, and we are doing it as a community. This means that communication is key, especially communication around our core principals. This is why we have crafted a Manifesto that guides our decision making and is therefore important to sign up to. You can read more about us on our website: jogl.io

How we work
